Wash your hands

Hand cleanliness is an important factor in reducing the risk of infection. Hands should be washed with soap and the duration of washing should be at least 20 seconds

Drink vitamins

Rospotrebnadzor experts advise taking multivitamins twice a day with meals - half a dose during breakfast and lunch or dinner. You should not drink them on an empty stomach - vitamins of group B and vitamin C in this case can cause pain in the stomach or nausea.

Wear a mask

Do not touch the mask during use; treat hands if touched. As soon as the mask gets damp, replace it with a new one. Do not reuse disposable masks.
